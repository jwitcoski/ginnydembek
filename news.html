NYC Parent Council Candidacy

I am excited to announce my candidacy for the NYC Parent Council. As both an educator and a parent, I am deeply committed to improving educational opportunities for all children in New York City, with a particular focus on supporting students with disabilities and their families.

My Platform

  • Inclusive Education: Advocating for truly inclusive educational environments that support the needs of all learners
  • Evidence-Based Practices: Promoting the implementation of research-supported instructional approaches across all schools
  • Parent Empowerment: Ensuring parents have the information, resources, and support they need to effectively advocate for their children
  • Equitable Resource Distribution: Working to ensure that all schools have the resources they need to support diverse learners
  • Transparent Decision-Making: Advocating for clear, transparent processes in educational policy decisions that affect our children

Get Involved

I welcome your support and input as I campaign for this important role. There are several ways you can get involved:

  • Share your concerns and priorities for NYC schools
  • Volunteer to help with campaign activities
  • Spread the word to other parents and community members
  • Attend upcoming community forums and events
